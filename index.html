About This Work
Exploring the intersection of urban decay and human presence through Portland's industrial landscapes.

This body of work documents the raw, unfiltered beauty found in forgotten spaces—abandoned factories, weathered infrastructure, and the quiet corners of Portland's industrial districts.

Through careful composition and a focus on texture, light, and atmosphere, each photograph reveals the poetry inherent in decay and transformation, inviting viewers to reconsider the overlooked spaces around them.

Mixed Media

Exploring abstraction, color theory, and gestural mark-making through layered compositions that blend digital and analog techniques.

Layered Composition

Mixed Media, 2024

Beyond Photography

My mixed media work explores the same themes as my photography—decay, transformation, layered histories—but through abstract visual language. Each piece builds through multiple passes: gestural marks, color fields, line work, and digital manipulation, creating dense visual narratives that reward extended viewing.

This practice informs my photographic eye, training me to see composition, color relationships, and visual rhythm in new ways.

Creative Process

01

Discovery & Exploration

Urban exploration and site research to identify compelling locations with strong visual narratives and historical significance.

02

Composition & Capture

Careful attention to light, texture, and framing. Each shot considers the relationship between architecture, decay, and human scale.

03

Post-Production

Selective color grading and contrast adjustments to enhance mood while maintaining authenticity and documentary integrity.
